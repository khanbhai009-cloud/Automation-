In compliance with Facebook Platform rules, you have the full right to request the deletion of your data. To remove your data and disconnect our app, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click Settings > Apps and Websites.
  3. Find our app in the list and click Remove.

Alternatively, you can email us directly to request the complete erasure of your data from our database.
